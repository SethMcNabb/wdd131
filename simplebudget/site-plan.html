Overview

Purpose

Simple Budget's purpose is just that, to be a simple online budget to help users make wise financial decisions without the headache of a more complicated budget application.

Audience

My audience is everyone who is tired of complicted budget applications and who wants the simplicity of a pen and paper budget in a didgital form. Many popular budget apps have users plan a whole months income and expenses in advance, which might work for some people, but many people don't make the same exact amount of money every month and could benefit from a program that helps them know how much they have available right now!

Dynamic elements

The dynamic elements provided by javascript are the ability to create budget categories, log expenses, view changing totals, delete specific expenses, and clear all data.

Content

Dashboard

The Dashboard page will display the users goals, Total Balance, Chosen Categories and balance, as well as their recent transactions. There will be a stock photo of a chart showing positive growth.

Expense Form

The second page will be an expense form for the user to use to record their expenses which will then adjust the totals alloted to each category they have already funded. There will be a generic stock image of money on this page.
